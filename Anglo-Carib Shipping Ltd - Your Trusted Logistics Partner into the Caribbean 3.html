Our Core Shipping Services

This section details the range of logistics solutions we offer. Whether you're a business shipping commercial goods or an individual moving personal effects, we have a service designed for your needs. Click on any card to reveal more information about how we can help.

Commercial Cargo

Sea & Air Freight solutions for businesses shipping inventory, equipment, and goods from anywhere in the world to Caribbean destinations.

We handle diverse commercial needs via Sea and Air, offering LCL, FCL, and air cargo consolidation. Our services cover all major ports and airports in the Caribbean, with full support for customs documentation.

Vehicle Shipping

Securely transport your cars, motorcycles, and vans from worldwide origins to various Caribbean ports via Sea Freight.

We manage all logistics for Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo) and containerized vehicle shipping. Our team will guide you through all necessary documentation and customs procedures.

Container Shipping

Full (FCL) and Less-than-Container Load (LCL) options for larger household moves or commercial cargo from worldwide origins.

Choose from 20ft or 40ft containers for exclusive use, or share container space with our economical LCL service for smaller, bulkier shipments originating globally.

Boxes & Crates

Ideal for personal effects or business items that don't require a full container, available via Sea or Air Freight to the Caribbean.

We provide competitive pricing for shipping boxes, crates, and pallets. This service is perfect for students, small businesses, or anyone sending smaller consignments.

Our Streamlined Process

To demystify international shipping, we've refined our global process into three transparent steps. This visual timeline walks you through the journey of your shipment, from initial contact to final delivery, ensuring you know what to expect at every stage.

Step 1: Get Your Quote

Fill out our simple online form with your shipment details, including origin and destination. We'll promptly provide a clear, no-obligation quote for Sea or Air Freight.

Step 2: Arrange Collection

Once you approve the quote, we'll coordinate collection from your worldwide address or guide you for drop-off at a designated partner depot. We handle the paperwork.

Step 3: Track & Receive

We ship your goods and provide a tracking number. Monitor your shipment's progress online until it's ready for collection at the destination port or airport in the Caribbean.

A New Standard in Caribbean Shipping

Established in 2024, Anglo-Carib Shipping Limited was founded to create a more personal, reliable, and straightforward shipping experience for Sea and Air Freight between worldwide origins and the Caribbean. As a new, owner-operated micro-entity, building an excellent reputation is paramount to us. We are committed to transparency and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our mission is simple: to be the most trusted shipping partner for the Caribbean diaspora and businesses needing Sea and Air Freight services from any global location to the Caribbean. We are proud to be the only UK shipping company offering a completely personalized end-to-end service. We understand the importance of every shipment and treat your goods as if they were our own.

Stephen Paul Woodard, Director, brings over 30 years of experience in the shipping and marine cargo insurance industry, ensuring your consignments are handled with unparalleled expertise and care.
